Your Commision Earnings Every Year

Without having to take on permanent new lines!

CommissionCrowd makes it easy to leverage your existing networks and maximize your sales commissions without having to take on new company principals on a permanent basis... here's how!

1
Leverage Your Existing Contacts

Meet with your regular clients and take the opportunity to ask if they need anything for their business, even if it's outside of your niche. Let them know you'll take care of it for them. You'll find the perfect suppliers, vet them on their behalf then make the introduction. Ask for specifics and you'll present relevant proposals to them shortly.

2
Find + Connect With The Perfect Vendors

Join CommissionCrowd or Login to your account if you're already a member (Note: It's 100% FREE for independent sales reps here's why)

Then use our sophisticated search filters to find the perfect companies who can service your client. All of our company members are pre-vetted and screened so you know you'll only be dealing with the best!

3
Agree A Commission % In Exchange For An Introduction

Once you've found the companies who can service your contact, let them know you're willing to make an introduction to your client. You can either choose to close the business yourself or simply make the introduction on their behalf and negotiate your referral fee or commission % when the deal closes. Use CommissionCrowd's functionality to easily manage your relationship with these companies, receive their proposals and any other important documents they share with you.

4
Introduce Both Parties To Eachother

Go back to your client and let them know you've found the perfect companies to fulfill their needs. Pass over their proposals and make the introduction. Set up the meeting if you don't want to close the deal yourself.

5
The Deal Closes

The deal closes and your client receives the products/services they were looking for. Note: Your sales pipeline and activity functionality within your CommissionCrowd account gives you a quick and easy way to see the stage each deal is at and your prospects can communicate with you each step of the way.

6
Get Paid Your Commission

The deal is done! Your client is happy and so is the company you referred. You get paid your commission even if you've not had/wanted to close the sale yourself.
